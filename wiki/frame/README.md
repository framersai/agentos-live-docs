## ğŸ”§ Technical Philosophy

### Open Source First
We believe in building in public and contributing to the community. Our core infrastructure is open source, enabling developers worldwide to build on our foundation.

### Local-First, Cloud-Ready
Privacy and ownership matter. Our tools work offline-first with optional cloud sync, ensuring users maintain control of their data.

### AI-Native Design
Built from the ground up for AI integration, not retrofitted. Every design decision considers how AI systems will interact with the platform.

### Standards-Based
We embrace open standards and protocols, ensuring interoperability and preventing vendor lock-in.
